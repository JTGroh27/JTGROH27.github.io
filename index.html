About Josh Groh

I am a senior at Southern Illinois University Edwardsville studying media production. I am 21-years-old and I originally am from Arnold, Missouri. I have two older brothers, Andy and Zach. I also have a cat named Meelo and he lives with me in my apartment here in Edwardsville, Illinois. Please enjoy looking around my website!
